I am a software engineer with a fascination with biological systems. In particular I like modeling biological systems.

In this project I incorporate modified hodgkins huxley dynamics.

Adding on to this there is an Isynapses and an Istimulus
Where Isynapses is the summation of all the synapse and Istimulus is the input stimulus.

The synapses are standard tsodyks markram synapses.
